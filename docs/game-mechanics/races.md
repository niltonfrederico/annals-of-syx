# Races

Eight inhabitable species. Each one has hardcoded preferences (food,
climate, building material, other races) that drive happiness, plus a
combat profile that drives military viability. A multi-race city is
mechanically a juggling act between these vectors — most race pairs
carry mutual happiness penalties, and a few pairs (Dondorian/Garthimi,
Tilapi/Garthimi) are nearly impossible to integrate.

Sub-classes within a population (Noble / Citizen / Slave / Child)
apply on top of the racial baseline and are covered in
[city-economy.md](city-economy.md#social-classes).

## Quick reference

| Race      | Climate     | Food prefs           | Breeds in       | Combat role          | Slave submission |
|-----------|-------------|----------------------|-----------------|----------------------|------------------|
| Human     | Temperate   | Bread, Meat, Mushrooms, Eggs | Nursery (Fruit) | Versatile infantry   | 1.00 |
| Cretonian | Temp/Warm   | Fruit, Veg, Bread, Mushrooms | Breeder (Veg)   | Unfit for combat     | 1.25 (highest) |
| Dondorian | Cold/Temp   | Meat, Fish, Mushrooms        | **Cannot breed** (immigration only) | Heavy infantry, hammers/plate | 0.75 (rebellious) |
| Tilapi    | Temp        | Meat, Bread, Fruit, Veg      | Nursery (Fruit) | Archers (peerless)   | 1.00 |
| Garthimi  | Temp/Warm   | Meat, Fish                   | Hatchery (Meat) — 16 days! | Swarm / cannon fodder | 1.00 |
| Amevia    | Needs water | Fish, Eggs, some Veg         | Slow aquatic    | Heavy shock infantry | Extremely low (won't submit) |
| Cantor    | (Haven gated) | Meat, Fish, Eggs           | Haven-only      | One-Cantor army      | Effectively un-enslaveable |
| Argonosh  | Mountains   | Meat, Fish, Eggs             | Haven-only      | Elite shock troops   | (They enslave others) |

## Per-race notes

### Human (`HUMAN`)

The default. Lifespan ~80y. 100% climate affinity in Temperate, 80% in
Cold/Warm. Best stats in intellect-bound work: labs, libraries,
administration. Universally tolerant — 1.00 happiness modifier
toward every other race. Nursery requires **Fruit** to raise infants
(80 in-game days = 5 years to adulthood).

Frequently enslaved by Tilapi and Garthimi specifically to fill
research/admin roles that those races cannot perform themselves. A
"Garthimi empire with 10,000 enslaved Human scribes" is a real and
common late-game macro.

### Cretonian (`CRETONIA`)

Pig-folk. **Strict vegetarians**. Breeder building consumes
**Vegetables**, not Fruit (this is the main confusion vs. Human/Tilapi
nurseries). Cubs starve fast if veg supply collapses. Cretonians
prefer wooden buildings (100%) and Temperate/Warm climates; despise
Cold (20%).

Unmatched farmers — massive innate bonuses to Cotton, Fruit, Grain,
Vegetables. Worthless in combat (cowardly, low morale). Highest slave
submission in the game (1.25) and **no negative race relations** —
the easiest race to integrate into any empire.

The HUMAN/CRETONIAN slave dynamic noted in
[`memory/project_human_race_slave_dynamics.md`](../../) lives here:
HUMAN players sell same-race slaves and buy DONDORIAN (which the
game's slave market actually calls "dwarves"), but for Cretonians the
common pattern is being *the* enslaved farmer in someone else's
empire.

### Dondorian (`DONDORI`)

Dwarves. **Spontaneously generated by the gods** on Mt. Cerebus — no
breeding building exists. A Dondorian-heavy city grows purely via
immigration, so a happiness crash equals demographic crash with no
recovery lever. Skip school entirely (no childhood).

Climate: 100% Temperate, 80% Cold, 10% Warm. Mountain housing
preference is +550%. Strong distaste for any wooden building.

Industry/military backbone. Mining, smelting, smithing, stone-cutting,
heavy infantry — all best-in-class. Atrocious farmers.

Hates Garthimi (-0.50), dislikes Tilapi (-0.25), enjoys killing
Amevia. Slave submission 0.75 — productive but rebellious.

### Tilapi (`TILAPI`)

Sylvan elves. Lifespan ~90y. Omnivorous (Meat, Bread, Fruit, Veg) and
they **eat their dead** — cannibalism is legal and incurs zero
happiness penalty. Nursery consumes **Fruit**, 48 in-game days to
adulthood (faster than Human, slower than Garthimi).

Peerless archers and bow-crafters. Strong bonuses to forestry,
husbandry, hunting. Average at mining/science — Tilapi empires
typically enslave Humans (for labs) and Dondorians (for forges).

Xenophobic toward Dondorian and Garthimi. Wants wooden buildings and
forest-adjacent settlements.

### Garthimi (`GARTHIMI`)

Insectoid swarm. **Fastest reproduction in the game**: hatchery turns
1 Meat/day into an adult in 16 in-game days. This is the entire
strategic identity — they can swarm-out of any losses.

Terrible at intellect, fine craftsmanship, agriculture. Good at mining,
fishing, and uniquely at Balticrawler ranching. Worship the fallen god
Aminion (some Shmalorism). Need round architecture (the engine
literally tracks per-room "roundness").

Individually weak in combat — bad damage and low morale — but the
exoskeleton gives natural armor, and the meatgrinder works at scale.
Mutual hatred with Dondorian. Highly tolerated as slaves (submission
1.00) and frequently bought as disposable mine labor.

### Amevia (`AMEVIA`)

Lizardmen / fish-folk hybrid. Recent addition. **Requires water** —
coastlines, rivers, wells, canals. A landlocked map is unplayable for
Amevia.

Diet: Fish, Eggs, some vegetables. Slow metabolism = low caloric drain
per capita. Unique: only race that can ranch **Globdiens** (egg
livestock) and the only race with a **Mud Wall** building option
(zero raw materials, slow to build).

Apathetic toward violence in lore, but mechanically formidable shock
infantry (natural armor + size). Slow breeders. **Extremely
non-submissive** — enslaving Amevia is a logistical nightmare; they
riot constantly. Best played isolationist; mixing with other races
in housing triggers immediate happiness debuffs.

### Cantor (`CANTOR`)

Giants. ~8000y lifespan. **Not playable as starting race** without
mods — acquired exclusively via the Haven mechanic: conquer the world
region containing a Cantor Haven, then meet draconian capital
prerequisites (≥1000 pop, 31% Faith, ≥36% Athurist population, 51% Law
coverage, 31% Awe, **zero Cantor slaves ever**). A single Cantor in
the player's possession permanently locks all future Cantor diplomacy.

Diet: Meat, Fish, Eggs. Strict Athurists — they demand absolute rule
of law and grand stone architecture. Notoriously picky on happiness;
a small dip in crime or food quality is enough to lose them.

One Cantor on the battlefield routinely shatters entire enemy
divisions via blunt-force damage.

### Argonosh (`ARGONO`)

Ancient demon-bugs, children of Shmalor. Haven-gated like Cantor.
Refuses all manual labor — only enjoys crime, punishment, law-related
work (executioners, wardens, slavers). Diet: Meat, Fish, Eggs;
cannibalism normalized to cope with their caloric demands.

Happiness driver is **Dread**, not Awe. A satisfied Argonosh city
looks like a horror — gibbets, execution blocks, slave pens. They
enslave everyone else (Humans for science, Garthimi for food/mining).
Extremely fast, devastating melee — a small Argonosh division breaks
lines wider than its headcount.

Haven prerequisites: ≥1000 pop, 31% Faith, 36% Food Storage, 21% Food
Rations, 16% Dread Access, ≥36% Shmalorist population.

## Race relations matrix (informal)

The engine tracks per-race "preference" toward each other race as a
multiplier on happiness when cohabiting. Key load-bearing edges:

- Dondorian ↔ Garthimi: **mutual hatred** (-0.50 from Dondorian side).
- Dondorian → Tilapi: -0.25.
- Dondorian → Amevia: actively hostile ("favourite occupation").
- Tilapi → Dondorian, Garthimi: dislike.
- Amevia → *everyone*: universal dislike (isolationist).
- Argonosh → Dondorian, Tilapi: hate.
- Human, Cretonian → everyone: 1.00 neutral (the only "glue" races).

For a save dumper, the practical takeaway is that a city's
"racial composition vector" is informative on its own: an all-Human
or all-Cretonian city implies stable cohesion; a Garthimi-Dondorian
mix implies ongoing happiness drag or active segregation.

## What to surface in dumps

Already covered by [`StatsDumper.populationByRaceAndClass`](../../src/main/java/dumper/StatsDumper.java)
and [`happinessByRace`](../../src/main/java/dumper/StatsDumper.java).
Candidates worth considering for expanded dumps:

- Per-race service satisfaction (food/clothing/coziness/religion).
- Per-race food consumption breakdown (validates preference alignment).
- Per-race slave count separated by source (bred vs. captured vs. bought).
- Race-relation tension score (sum of negative cohabitation modifiers).
